About Us

Launched in KSR College of Engineering, Preteen has grown from a home project to one of the largest food aggregators in the KSR. We are present in 6+ Canteen in KSR, enabling our vision of better food for more people by extending our reach. We not only connect people to food in every context but work closely with restaurants to enable a sustainable ecosystem.
