FAQ's

1. Is it necessary to sign up/log in for ordering food?
Yes, it is mandatory to create an account and log in to place an order.

2. How many restaurants are available for ordering?
Currently, only 1 restaurant is available, but we plan to expand soon.

3. How do I add food items to my cart?
Go to the "Menu" page, select items, and they will be added to your cart.

4. Can I pay with a debit card?
Yes, we accept debit, credit, and ATM cards.

5. Can I cancel my order after payment?
Sorry, order cancellations are not available at the moment.

6. Can I change my account details?
Yes, you can change your username and password.

7. How can I contact the restaurant?
Our email and contact number are mentioned at the bottom of the website.

8. Can I repeat my previous order?
Yes, your last order items will be saved in the cart for easy reordering.
